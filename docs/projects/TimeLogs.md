# Time Logs

## What is a Time Log?
A **Time Log** tracks the amount of time spent on cards, tasks, or projects. Time logs help teams measure effort, analyze productivity, and support billing or reporting.

## Use Cases
- Track time spent on specific cards or tasks
- Analyze team or individual productivity
- Support client billing and project costing

## User Actions
- Create, edit, and delete time logs
- Assign time logs to cards, tasks, or users
- View and export time tracking reports

## How Time Logs Work Together
- Time logs provide insight into project effort and resource allocation
- Accurate time tracking supports better planning and reporting
