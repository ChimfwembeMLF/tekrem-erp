# Templates

## What is a Template?
A **Template** is a reusable structure for cards, boards, or projects. Templates help teams standardize processes and quickly create new work items with predefined fields and checklists.

## Use Cases
- Standardize common workflows (e.g., bug report, feature request)
- Quickly create new cards, boards, or projects
- Ensure consistency across teams and projects

## User Actions
- Create, edit, and delete templates
- Apply templates to new cards, boards, or projects
- Customize templates for different use cases

## How Templates Work Together
- Templates save time and reduce errors by providing ready-made structures
- Teams can share and reuse templates across projects
