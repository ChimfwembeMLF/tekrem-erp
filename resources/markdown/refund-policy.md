# Refund Policy

## 1. Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase if the product is unused and in its original condition.

## 2. Process

To request a refund, contact our support team at support@tekrem.com with your order details. We will review the request and process the refund within 7–10 business days.

## 3. Exceptions

Refunds are not offered for digital downloads, custom orders, or services that have already been delivered.

## 4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted for partially used services or items with minor defects, at the discretion of Tekrem Innovation.

## 5. Legal Rights

This Refund Policy does not affect any statutory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws.
